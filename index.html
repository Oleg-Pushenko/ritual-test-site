  • Polymorphisms. A variant in your DNA sequence, which is hard wired before you are born. The most common ones when it comes to nutrition are Vitamin D, Omega-3s, Folate and B12. What happens when you have them is you make a protein that is less active than it should be (basically, that’s not good). You have a harder time utilizing those nutrients from your food, or the sun from vitamin D in that case. The good news is supplementation 🙌 , especially the right kind, can assist with nutrient intake, despite most polymorphisms [i.e., D3 vs. D2, MTHF vs. folic acid, DHA vs. ALA]
